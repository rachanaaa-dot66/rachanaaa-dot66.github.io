Cambodia Banking Sector Risk Analysis

Analyzed Cambodia's banking sector using NBC Annual Reports (2020–2024), Yahoo Finance market data, and IMF indicators — covering NPL deterioration, concentration risk, liquidity vulnerability, capital adequacy, and market shock transmission across 40+ banks.

Key Finding

System NPL rose from 2.0% → 7.2% (2020–2024), breaching the Basel 5% threshold. 8 banks exceed 10% NPL, with regional market stress leading credit deterioration by 12–18 months.

04 / 05Statistical Analysis

Health Insurance Charges — Statistical Analysis

Investigated whether gender drives health insurance pricing using progressive regression modeling across 1,338 individuals — from unadjusted t-test through full multivariate control for age, BMI, smoking, children, and region. Conducted in R with full diagnostic checking.

Key Finding

Men initially pay $1,387 more (p=0.036), but this disappears entirely after controlling for BMI and smoking (p=0.745). Primary driver: smoking adds $23,834. R² jumps from 0.3% → 75.1% when health factors are added.

05 / 05Business Analytics

Logistics Delivery Performance Analysis

Cleaned and analyzed 10,999 orders in Excel (pivot tables), then built an interactive Power BI dashboard tracking delivery performance across weight categories, warehouses, shipment modes, and discount tiers — identifying key drivers of the 40.3% late delivery rate.

Key Finding

40.3% of 10,999 orders were delivered late (4,436 orders). Heavy and Medium weight categories had the highest delay rates, and low-discount orders showed the most delays — average order cost $210.20.
